ABOUT ME

I AM A CONTENT CREATOR 

Human expression is a marvelous and necessary action. The impulse to share other people's perspectives including my own, courses through my veins and inspires me to create. I am a visually oriented person, meaning I conceptualize the big picture and envision what my end goal should look like. I am confident in my abilities but cognizant of the fact that there's always more to learn. My aesthetic technique has been described as "immersive" and "dank sauce." Music brings me closer to my emotions and motivates my inclinations. I DJ in hopes of doing the same for others. Browse around, stay for a while.
