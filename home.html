ABOUT ME

Visionary (noun):
a person with original ideas about what the future will or could be like.

Creative expression has taken the driver's seat my whole life. Ever since I started experimenting with digital imagery as a middle schooler, the impulse to create and share my ideas has absorbed my attention and fueled my ambitions. I show a great deal of repect for those who have climbed the mountains I intend to conquer. I belong in this industry because I do whatever it takes to manifest a vision. Being nocturnal helps too.

I'm visually oriented when it comes to stepping back and conceptualizing the full frame. From there I excell at contemplating fine details that are often overlooked. I'm confident in my abilities but cognizant of the fact that there's always more for me to learn. 

Music ignites my imagination which intern fuels my stride and swagger as a creative indivisual. I DJ to activate Sublime feelings and spread the invigorating vibes I feel when mixing / making music.
