ABOUT ME

A CREATOR OF VISUAL CONTENT

Human expression has always been a driving force in the life I so boldly live. The impulse to create and share sensational content with the world reigns first in command over my throughts and inspires me to create visionary works like the ones you see around you here. I'm visually oriented, meaning I conceptualize the big picture and envision the checkpoints I need to reach in order to successfully accomplish my goals. I'm confident in my abilities but also cognizant of the fact that there's always more to orient myself with. Ambition is my not so secret ingredient for producing content. Intuition is my go-to problem solving procedure.
Music motivates my day-to-day inclinations which brings me closer to my thoughts and emotions. I DJ to induce positive vibes in others and promote Sublime atmospheres around us.
